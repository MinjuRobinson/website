THINGS TO DO

Biking track on the Queenscliff

Walk this track from Topeka bay to the central Queenscliff which runs high above State Highway 1 and the railway line, reaching 220 metres above sea level. There are spectacular views, and it is one of the highlights of the Te Araroa Trail.

Swimming at the Sandy beach

Here, enjoy swimming in Sandy Beach, where the water is very clear and warm. It is too safe for children to swim because the water is not deep. It is a beach swimming that the whole family can enjoy. It's a great place to relax on a very fine and soft sandy beach. There is a cafe 5 minutes walk distance.

Sunday market

Queenclffe's longest-running 'farmers style' Market (over 21years) featuring a great selection of Queenclffe producers, makers, bakers and growers. Runs every Saturday from 7.30am to 12.30pm in Moana Street and behind the Harvey's building beside the carpark.

PLACE TO EAT

Seafood restaurant

It is a 50-year-old seafood restaurant that only cooks seafood harvested from Queencliffe. All the ingredients are very fresh and can be BYO. The opening hours are from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. every day. Reservations are required.

Sandy Beach bakery

It is one of the famous bakeries in New Zealand and serves only freshly baked bread every day. It is a local attraction and displays 100-year-old traditional baking equipment in the bakery. You can see the history of baking and taste delicious bread. Freshly baked bread can be tasted at 7 a.m. and 2 p.m.
